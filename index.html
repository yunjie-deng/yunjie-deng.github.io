Yunjie Deng

Atlanta, GA, USA

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the School of Cybersecurity and Privacy at Georgia Institute of Technology, advised by Sukarno Mertoguno, starting from Fall 2023.

My research interests lie in System Security, GPU Security, and Hardware-assisted Security.

Prior to joining Georgia Tech, I earned my M.Eng. degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech) in 2023, advised by Prof. Fengwei Zhang. I also obtained my B.Eng. degree in Computer Science and Engineering from SUSTech in 2020, advised by Prof. Xin Yao.
